# Reqvolt Specification

## 1. Executive Summary

Reqvolt is an AI-powered SaaS platform for agile teams that converts messy discovery inputs into evidence-linked Story Packs. It transforms meeting notes, emails, documents, customer feedback, and workshop outputs into testable acceptance criteria with QA quality gates, stakeholder review workflows, and one-click push to Monday.com.

## 2. Target Users & Value Proposition

### Target Users
- Product Owners
- Product Managers
- Business Analysts
- Scrum Masters
- Anyone responsible for backlog quality in agile teams

### Value Proposition
- **From mess to method**: Converts unstructured discovery inputs into structured, evidence-linked stories
- **Evidence-backed**: Every story and acceptance criterion links to source material
- **QA quality gates**: Deterministic rules + AI auto-fix for vague terms, untestable criteria
- **Stakeholder review**: Shareable links with comments, no login required
- **One-click export**: Push to Monday.com, export DOCX
